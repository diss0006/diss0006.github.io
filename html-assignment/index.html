About Me

My name is Emith Hansana. This is the 20th year since I appeared on Earth. I am currently enrolled in "Interactive Media Design" program in Algonquin College. I was born and raised in Yakkala, Sri Lanka. My main three obsessions are Music, Cars and motorcycles, But I also enjoy playing video games with friends and travelling.

I aim to build myself a well balanced life. My goal is to make my career out of my passion, and that is to be a music producer. Hopefully, Someday I will be the G.O.A.T of the music industry. But Above all, I will make sure that I will stay happy and satisfied with who I am right now, until I will be who I want to be.
